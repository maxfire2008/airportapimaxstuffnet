Opening Hours

Due to the changes to flight services as a result of COVID-19, the terminal at Hobart Airport is currently open three hours prior to the first scheduled departure and closes 30 minutes after the last scheduled departure each day.

If there are changes to arrival or departure times, we will adjust our opening hours to accommodate.
